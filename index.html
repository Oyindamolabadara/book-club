About Us

Book sitters club is a close knit community of book lovers and book enthusiasts. Started in 2020 by two friends over a cup of coffee, BSC is a one-stop shop for people who love reading, talking and breathing books. At Book Sitters Club, we believe that we can develop meaningful relationships from our love of words and books. Our interests cover all genre and cater to every single type of book lover out there. Our mission is to foster deep connections with not only words but with one other people. We meet up every last Saturdays of the month at InstaCoffee Cafe.
